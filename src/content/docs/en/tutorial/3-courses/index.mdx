Now that you've created activity modules and personalized your profile, it's time to organize your content into courses and manage student access!

## Looking ahead

In this unit, you'll learn how to create and manage **courses** in Paideia LMS. You'll build:

- A course with organized sections and categories
- Course content by adding modules to sections
- Student access through enrolments
- Student organization using groups

Along the way, you'll learn how to structure course content, manage student access, and organize students into groups for collaborative activities.
