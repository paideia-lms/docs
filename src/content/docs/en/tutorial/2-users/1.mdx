Now that you're logged into Paideia LMS, it's time to personalize your account by updating your user profile!

<PreCheck>
  - Access your user profile settings
  - Upload an avatar image
  - Update your profile information (bio, name, etc.)
</PreCheck>

## Access your profile

<Steps>
1. Navigate to your user profile. You can usually find this by clicking on your name or avatar in the top navigation bar.

2. Click on "Edit Profile" or "Settings" to access your profile editing page.

   You should see fields for:
   - **First Name** and **Last Name**
   - **Email** (usually read-only)
   - **Bio** - A short description about yourself
   - **Avatar** - Your profile picture
   - **Theme** - Light or dark mode preference
</Steps>

## Upload an avatar

<Steps>
3. Upload an avatar image:

   - Click on the avatar upload area or "Change Avatar" button
   - Select an image file from your computer (common formats: JPG, PNG, GIF)
   - Crop or adjust the image if needed
   - Save your changes

   :::tip[Avatar best practices]
   - Use a square image for best results
   - Recommended size: 200x200 pixels or larger
   - Keep file size reasonable (under 2MB)
   :::

4. Verify that your avatar appears correctly in your profile and in the navigation bar.
</Steps>

## Update your profile information

<Steps>
5. Update your profile details:

   - **First Name** and **Last Name**: Make sure these are correct
   - **Bio**: Add a brief description about yourself, your role, or your interests
   - **Theme**: Choose your preferred theme (light or dark mode)

6. Save your changes by clicking the "Save" or "Update Profile" button.

7. Verify your changes by viewing your profile page.
</Steps>

:::note[Profile visibility]

Your profile information is visible to other users in the system. The avatar and name are typically visible to all users, while other details may vary based on your privacy settings.
:::

Congratulations! Your profile is now personalized and ready!
